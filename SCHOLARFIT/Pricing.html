Frequently Asked Question

What is included in the free Trial.

The free Trial include access to all subjects in the UTME syllabus, Two practice tests with results and analysis, and basic AI-powered recommendations. However, you are limited to initially selected subjects with no changes allowed.

Can I change subjects in the premium plan?

Yes, the premium plan allows you to change subjects at any time, providing flexibility in your learning journey.

What are the benefits of the enterprise plan?

The enterprise plan includes all premium features for each student, a centralized management dashboard for administrators, bulk student enrollment and management, detailed progress reports for groups and individuals, customizeable learning paths for different students, and a dedicated account manager with premium support.
