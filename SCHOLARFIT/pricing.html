Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the Free Trial?

The Free Trial includes access to all UTME subjects, two practice tests with results and analysis, and basic AI-powered recommendations. However, you are limited to the initially selected subjects with no changes allowed.

Can I change subjects in the Premium plan?

Yes, the Premium plan allows you to change subjects anytime, providing flexibility in your learning journey.

What are the benefits of the Enterprise plan?

The Enterprise plan includes all Premium features for each student, a centralized management dashboard, bulk student enrollment and management, detailed progress reports, and more.
