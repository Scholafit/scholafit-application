Exam Completion

  • The submit button remains disabled unitl all questions are answered
  • When enabled the submit button will appear in purple
  • In the event time runs out before submitting, a pop up will appear prompting you to submit

Important Notes

  • Do not refresh or close the browser window during the exam
  • Answer all questions before the time limit expires
  • Your 2 hour timer will begin immediately after clicking the 'Start Exam' button
